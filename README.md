## 📌 Problem Statement

Organizations often struggle to understand **why employees leave** and which factors contribute most to attrition. Without proper analysis, companies may:

- Lose high-performing employees  
- Face increased hiring and training costs  
- Experience reduced productivity  
- Fail to identify at-risk employee groups  

This project analyzes HR data to uncover **patterns in attrition, job satisfaction, and workforce demographics**.

---

## ❓ Key Business Questions

- What is the overall attrition rate?  
- Which departments experience the highest employee turnover?  
- Which age groups are most likely to leave?  
- How does job satisfaction impact attrition?  
- Are there differences in attrition across gender or education fields?  

---

## 📊 Project Overview

This dashboard provides insights into employee data, including:

- Attrition trends  
- Department-wise analysis  
- Age group distribution  
- Job satisfaction ratings  
- Education field impact  

It enables HR teams to make **data-driven decisions for improving retention**.

## 🔍 Key Insights

- 📉 **Attrition Rate is 16.12%**, indicating moderate retention challenges  
- 🏢 **R&D department has the highest attrition (56%)**, making it the most critical area  
- 👥 **Employees aged 25–34 show the highest turnover**, highlighting early-career attrition  
- 👶 **Under 25 employees also show notable attrition**, suggesting onboarding issues  
- 😊 **Low job satisfaction correlates with higher attrition**, especially in Sales and Research roles  
- 🎓 **Life Sciences and Medical fields experience higher attrition**, indicating strong external demand  
- 📊 **Sales roles contribute significantly to attrition**, likely due to high-pressure environments  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- 🎯 Focus retention strategies on **25–34 age group employees**  
- 🏢 Improve **R&D work environment and career growth opportunities**  
- 👶 Strengthen **onboarding and early employee engagement programs**  
- 😊 Improve **job satisfaction through feedback and recognition systems**  
- 💰 Benchmark **compensation for high-demand roles** in life science and medical fields
- 🎯 Improve work life balance in **Research Scientist, sales executive, laboratory technicians**   
- 📊 Use **predictive analytics to identify at-risk employees early**
